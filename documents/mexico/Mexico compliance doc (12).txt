12. Regional Market Compliance Notes
12.1 Mexico City (CDMX)
* Outside the restricted zone — direct title available to all foreign buyers.
* ISAI is progressive: can reach 4–8% for higher-value properties.
* No formal AMPI MLS — entirely portal-driven.
* Portal Inmuebles24 is dominant. Propiedades.com is strong in residential.
* CDMX is Mexico's most regulated rental market — verify current rent control and housing protections under the Ley de Vivienda for long-term rental listings.


12.2 Riviera Maya — Cancún, Playa del Carmen, Tulum (Quintana Roo)
* Entire market is within the 50km coastal restricted zone — fideicomiso required for all foreign buyers.
* ISAI: 2% (among the lowest in Mexico).
* ISH (lodging tax): 5% on short-term rental income in Quintana Roo.
* IVA on short-term rentals: 16% on gross income.
* Tulum: significant ejido land risk — buyers and platforms must verify land classification with National Agrarian Registry (RAN) before listing or transacting.
* New hotel zone developments (e.g., Tulum hotel zone) may require environmental impact permits and SEMARNAT approvals.


12.3 Los Cabos (Baja California Sur)
* Entire market is within the 50km coastal restricted zone — fideicomiso required for all foreign buyers.
* ISAI: 3%.
* Highest average sale prices in Mexico for foreign buyers after some CDMX neighbourhoods.
* Los Cabos MLS is the dominant data platform — AMPI Los Cabos affiliated.
* Strong U.S. and Canadian buyer base — listings should ideally be bilingual.


12.4 Puerto Vallarta / Riviera Nayarit
* Coastal restricted zone applies — fideicomiso required for foreign residential buyers.
* Vallarta-Nayarit MLS is the most established regional MLS in Mexico, operating since 1988.
* Dual-state market: Jalisco (Vallarta) and Nayarit — different state tax rates may apply depending on property side.
* AMPI Vallarta, AMPI Riviera Nayarit, and AMPI Compostela all operate in this market.


12.5 Monterrey / Nuevo León
* Major industrial and business city. Not in the restricted zone.
* Direct foreign ownership permitted.
* ISAI: 2–3%.
* No regional MLS — portal-driven.
* Strong nearshoring investment demand driving commercial and residential activity in 2025–2026.
