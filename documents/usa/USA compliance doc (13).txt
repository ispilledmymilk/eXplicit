15. AI, AVM, and Digital Staging Compliance
15.1 AVM Data Sourcing Constraints
* Training an AVM requires historical sold data. In non-disclosure states, this must be sourced exclusively via authorized VOW feeds.
* Platforms are strictly prohibited from scraping sold data from county websites or competitor portals for AVM training.
* Sellers may opt out of AVM display using the AutomatedValuationModelYN flag in the MLS feed Platforms must respect the opt-out flag per individual property on every page render.
15.2 AI-Enhanced Photo Disclosure
* The 2026 compliance standard requires clear labeling of virtually staged or AI-enhanced photos.
* RESO Data Dictionary 2.2 introduces the MediaDigitalEnhancementYN field to automate this disclosure.
* Marketplace displays should provide a side-by-side or alternating view of original and AI-staged photos.
* The OriginalMediaUrl flag in the RESO feed provides access to the non-altered source image.
