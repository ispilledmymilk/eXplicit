3. Agent Licensing — The AMI License
All real estate agents and agencies operating in Portugal must hold a valid AMI license (Licença AMI) issued by IMPIC. Operating without a valid AMI license is illegal and exposes the individual or company to civil and criminal penalties. For marketplace platforms, the AMI license is the single most important credential to verify when onboarding agency partners.

3.2 Verifying an AMI License
Platforms must verify each agency's AMI status before granting listing access. The process is straightforward: visit impic.pt, navigate to 'Verificar número AMI', enter the license number, and confirm active status and valid insurance. An AMI number that returns no result or shows 'suspended' status must not be onboarded.
3.3 APEMIP — Professional Association
The Associação dos Profissionais e Empresas de Mediação Imobiliária de Portugal (APEMIP) is a voluntary professional association for real estate practitioners. APEMIP membership is not required for legal operation — IMPIC registration is the mandatory credential. However, APEMIP membership signals professional commitment and adherence to a code of ethics.
