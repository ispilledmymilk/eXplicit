Portugal Real Estate
Compliance Policy 2026
Expert Edition for Real Estate Marketplace Platforms
Verified & Updated — March 16, 2026
DOCUMENT PURPOSE: This document is the authoritative compliance reference for real estate marketplace platforms operating in Portugal as of March 2026. It covers agent licensing, portal rules, taxes, Alojamento Local regulations, privacy law, and transaction processes.
CRITICAL STRUCTURAL DIFFERENCE: Portugal has NO Multiple Listing Service (MLS). There is no IDX, VOW, or centralized listing database equivalent to the U.S. system. Listings are distributed by agencies and private sellers independently across competing commercial portals.
