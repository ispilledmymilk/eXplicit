9. Municipal AL Containment Zones
While the national freeze on new AL licences was lifted in early 2025, individual municipalities retain the power to declare 'containment zones' where new registrations are restricted or blocked. The rules vary significantly by city and are subject to change. Platforms must verify current containment zone status before processing any new AL registration in a restricted area.
CRITICAL: Always verify current containment zone status directly with the relevant municipality before processing an AL application. Rules change and what was open in one quarter may be blocked in the next.


9.1 Lisbon
Lisbon's AL regulatory history is important context for any platform processing historical or current AL applications. On 5 November 2024, Lisbon suspended all new AL registrations citywide. The suspension was extended in May 2025 while the municipality revised its AL regulation. The updated regulation was then formally adopted in November 2025, replacing the blanket suspension with the structured containment zone thresholds described below. Any AL application submitted between November 2024 and November 2025 should be flagged for manual status review.
* Absolute containment trigger: 10% AL ratio at parish or neighborhood level — no new licences permitted.
* Relative containment range: 5–10% AL ratio — restricted and monitored.
* Current citywide ratio: approximately 7.2% (as of March 2026).


Lisbon Parishes/Neighborhoods — New AL Registrations Currently BLOCKED
* Parishes: Santa Maria Maior, Misericórdia, Santo António, São Vicente, Arroios, Estrela
* Neighborhoods: Ajuda, Alcântara, Belém, Bom Sucesso, Parque das Nações, Picoas, São Bento, São Sebastião da Pedreira, Sapadores
* Additional restricted pockets in Alvalade (Entrecampos, Av. Gago Coutinho), Marvila, and Beato


9.2 Porto
* Regulation adopted: 10 December 2024.
* Absolute containment threshold: 15% AL ratio per parish.
* New licences blocked in historic center parishes (Bonfim, Cedofeita, Miragaia, etc.).
* Temporary licences may be possible in non-saturated areas — verify per-parish with Porto municipality.
