1. Market Structure and Fundamental Differences
The Portuguese real estate market operates on a fundamentally different architecture from the U.S. MLS-based system. There is no centralized listing database, no mandatory data sharing protocol between competing agencies, and no national IDX or VOW framework. Listings are distributed by individual agencies and private sellers across a fragmented ecosystem of competing commercial portals, with each agency choosing which portals to list on and under what terms.
