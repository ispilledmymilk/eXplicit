13. Visa and Residency — Property Context
IMPORTANT: Property purchase alone does NOT grant residency or citizenship in Portugal. The following visas are available to foreign buyers but are separate from the purchase itself.


Visa / Programme
	Description
	Property Requirement
	2026 Status
	Golden Visa (ARI)
	Residency by investment. Now limited to non-residential/commercial real estate.
	Residential property in Lisbon, Porto, or most coastal areas NO LONGER qualifies for ARI (since 2022 reform).
	Active but restricted. Verify current eligible investment categories with AIMA.
	D7 Passive Income Visa
	For retirees and passive income earners. No investment threshold.
	None — but property ownership is common among applicants.
	Active
	D8 Digital Nomad Visa
	For remote workers employed by non-Portuguese companies or clients.
	None required.
	Active
	NHR / IFICI
	Tax incentive for new residents. NHR replaced by IFICI in 2024.
	None — tax residence based on habitual residence.
	IFICI active from 2024. NHR closed for new applicants.
