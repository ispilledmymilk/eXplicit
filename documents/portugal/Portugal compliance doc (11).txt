12. Key Property Documents Reference
Portuguese Name
	English Name
	Issued By
	Purpose
	Certidão Permanente
	Permanent Land Registry Certificate
	Conservatória do Registo Predial (Land Registry)
	Proves ownership and any encumbrances, mortgages, or debts. Available online via predial.pt.
	Caderneta Predial
	Property Tax Booklet
	AT — Portal das Finanças
	Shows official VPT, property classification, and area data.
	Licença de Utilização
	User / Habitation Licence
	Municipality (Câmara Municipal)
	Confirms building is approved for intended use. Required for buildings built after August 1951.
	Ficha Técnica de Habitação
	Building Data Sheet
	Developer/architect — filed with municipality
	Technical specifications. Required if user licence was issued after 30 March 2004.
	Certificado Energético
	Energy Performance Certificate (EPC)
	Certified energy auditor (perito qualificado)
	MANDATORY for all property sales and rental listings. Classes A+ (best) to F (worst). Valid for 10 years.
	Certidão de Infraestruturas
	Infrastructure Certificate
	Municipality
	Required if a Plot Partitioning Licence was registered since 1992.
	Procuração
	Power of Attorney
	Notary
	Authorises a representative (typically a lawyer) to sign on the buyer's behalf. Common for remote purchases.
	NIF
	Fiscal Identification Number
	AT — Finanças office
	Required before any contract, bank account opening, or property ownership. Mandatory for all buyers, sellers, and AL operators.
