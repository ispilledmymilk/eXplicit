14. Universal Compliance Checklist
A mature compliance programme for a Portugal marketplace platform must integrate the following controls across technical, legal, and operational functions.
Area
	Verification Step
	Frequency
	Agent AMI License
	Verify AMI number active on IMPIC. Confirm insurance valid.
	On agency onboarding
	AMI Display
	AMI number shown on all agency listings on all portals.
	Per listing
	Energy Certificate
	Valid EPC present. Class displayed on listing. Check expiry.
	Per listing
	NIF Verification
	Buyer and seller NIF obtained before contract signing.
	Per transaction
	Certidão Permanente
	Land registry certificate pulled. No blocking encumbrances.
	Before CPCV
	IMT Payment
	IMT paid via Portal das Finanças before deed signing.
	Before Escritura
	Stamp Duty
	0.8% paid to notary at deed signing.
	At Escritura
	Land Registry Filing
	Ownership registered post-deed at Conservatória.
	After Escritura
	AL License
	AL registration number obtained and displayed on STR listing.
	Before first guest
	AL Insurance
	Valid commercial landlord insurance. Proof within 3 days if requested.
	Annual check
	AL Guest Registration
	All non-Portuguese guests registered via SIBA (AIMA) within 3 days.
	Per stay
	Tourist Tax
	Correct rate collected and remitted monthly to correct municipality.
	Monthly
	AL Containment Check
	Verify property not in municipal containment zone before AL application.
	Before AL application
	GDPR Consent
	Marketing opt-in obtained before listing alerts or newsletters sent.
	Before first send
	Cookie Consent
	Explicit consent banner active and compliant on platform.
	Live on site
	DPO Registration
	DPO contact registered with CNPD if company is required to designate one.
	At designation
