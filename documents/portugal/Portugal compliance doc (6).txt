6. Standard Transaction Process
The standard Portuguese property purchase follows a defined sequence. Platforms building workflow tools, compliance checkers, or transaction management features must reflect this structure accurately.
Step
	Action
	Party
	Key Compliance Point
	1
	Obtain NIF (fiscal number)
	Buyer
	Required before any contract, bank account, or property ownership. Foreign buyers obtain at Finanças office or via lawyer with power of attorney.
	2
	Property search via portals
	Buyer
	Verify agent's AMI number on IMPIC before engaging. Check insurance status.
	3
	Legal due diligence
	Buyer's lawyer
	Pull Certidão Permanente (land registry certificate). Check for mortgages, debts, easements. Verify caderneta predial.
	4
	Sign CPCV + pay deposit
	Buyer + Seller
	Deposit (10–30%) held in lawyer's escrow. Double-deposit penalty applies for seller default.
	5
	Pay IMT via Portal das Finanças
	Buyer
	Must be paid before deed signing. Notary will not proceed without proof of payment.
	6
	Pay Stamp Duty
	Buyer
	Flat 0.8% of purchase price (or VPT if higher). Paid to notary at deed signing.
	7
	Sign Escritura at notary
	Buyer + Seller + Notary
	Balance paid by certified bank cheque. Notary issues deed.
	8
	Register at Land Registry + AT
	Buyer's lawyer
	Conservatória do Registo Predial. Also notify AT of new ownership.
	9
	IMI begins next calendar year
	Buyer (new owner)
	IMI billed to owner as of 31 December of purchase year. First bill arrives in the following year.
	

6.1 Required Documents for Deed Signing
The notary will require all of the following documents to be in order before proceeding with the Escritura. Platforms building transaction management tools should use this as a document checklist:
* Civil and tax identification documents of all parties
* Certidão Permanente (land registry extract — proves ownership and any encumbrances)
* Caderneta Predial (property tax booklet from AT — shows VPT and official property data)
* Licença de Utilização (user/habitation licence — required for buildings constructed after August 1951)
* Ficha Técnica de Habitação (building data sheet — required if user licence issued after 30 March 2004)
* Certificado Energético (energy performance certificate — mandatory for all transactions)
* Certidão de Infraestruturas (if Plot Partitioning Licence registered since 1992)
* Proof of IMT payment
* Proof of Stamp Duty payment
* CPCV (if signed)
